Placement Procedure

01

Registration and Application

Interested students register for the placement process through the college's online portal or designated registration forms.

02

Shortlisting

Based on the criteria set by companies, the placement cell shortlists candidates whose profiles match the job requirements.

03

Selection Rounds

candidates are evaluated based on their skills, knowledge, communication abilities, and overall suitability for the job role.

04

Offer Letters and Placement

Successful candidates receive offer letters from the companies detailing all the terms & conditions
