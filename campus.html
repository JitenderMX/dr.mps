Library

Our library boasts over 25,000 textbooks and 5,000 reference books, supplemented by a wide array of national and international journals. We offer numerous e-resources in the form of CDs, DVDs, and online subscriptions. Additionally, each faculty maintains separate libraries tailored to the specific needs of their respective courses.
